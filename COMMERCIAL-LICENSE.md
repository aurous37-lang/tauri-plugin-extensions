## Honest scope (as of this revision)

So you can evaluate before buying: the plugin currently loads **Phantom**
(Solana + EVM) and **Rabby** (EVM) on **Windows x86_64 (WebView2)**, reaching a
live `eth_chainId` response for both. **MetaMask is not supported** in this
version (a documented, architecture-level blocker). macOS and Linux backends
are stubs. Evaluate against your actual requirements under the noncommercial
license first.
